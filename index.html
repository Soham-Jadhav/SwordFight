Sword Fight


Engage in intense battles in this multiplayer-player fighting game.
Choose your player, and strategically defeat your opponent to claim victory in dynamic, adrenaline-fueled duels.

For player 1: A to move left, D to move right, W to jump, Space to attack

For player 2: Left arrow to move left, Right arrow to move right, Up arrow to jump, Down arrow to attack
